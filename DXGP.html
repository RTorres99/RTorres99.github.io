Project Information

  • Duration: November 2020 - December 2020
  • Team Size: 1
  • Platform: Windows PC
  • Tool: DirectX
  • Type: Solo-Project

Concept

This project was made so I could practice low-level development within DirectX.

My Contribution

  • Loaded in several pre-made and personal FBX models, including a several planets, a space station, a spaceship, and a skybox to create a solar system scene
  • Used 3D math in order to make the planets to orbit around a central sun, as well as spin on their own axis
  • Used HLSL shaders to create spectral reflections, alpha transparency, alpha blending, and glowing emission textures
  • Created a controllable FPS-style camera to move about the scene
  • Lighting, including Directional, Point, Spotlight, and Ambient
  • Loaded in a sphere that actively reflects the skybox
  • Made a second viewport in order to provide a camera-fixed minimap in the corner
  • Utilized CMake to generate the project files for editing and debugging
