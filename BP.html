Balloon Popper

C++

Project Information

  • Duration: 6 hours
  • Team Size: 1
  • Platform: Windows PC
  • Tool: DirectX
  • Type: Solo-Project

Concept

This project was meant to test me on the concepts I learned from my DirectX Graphics Project. I was given a time limit of just 6 hours to complete a set of requirments for this project. This included lighting, poppable balloons, and a crossbow attachted to the player.

My Contribution

  • Loaded FBX models for the balloons, crossbow, plain, and skybox
  • Created an FPS camera that was locked on the Y as to not allow the player to fly
  • Made shaders for the spectral reflections on the crossbow and the balloons
  • Allowed the balloons to be popped with a click of the mouse, as long as they are under the crosshair
  • Directional and Ambient lighting
  • Made the balloons move in a sin-wave pattern, as well as made them slowly blow back up after being popped
  • Created an infinite skybox, to give the player a much more interesting environment
